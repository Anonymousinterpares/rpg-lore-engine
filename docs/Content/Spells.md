# Content: Spells (Core List)

**Name** | Lvl | School | Time | Range | Comp | Duration | Effect

## 1. Magic Rules & Progression

### The 8 Schools of Magic
*   **Abjuration:** Protective spells. *Shield, Mage Armor, Dispel Magic.*
*   **Conjuration:** Transportation and creation. *Misty Step, Web, Find Familiar.*
*   **Divination:** Revealing information. *Detect Magic, Identify, Scrying.*
*   **Enchantment:** Mental influence. *Charm Person, Sleep, Hold Person.*
*   **Evocation:** Elemental energy. *Fireball, Magic Missile, Cure Wounds.*
*   **Illusion:** Deception to senses. *Invisibility, Disguise Self, Mirror Image.*
*   **Necromancy:** Life, death, and undeath. *Animate Dead, Revivify, Spare the Dying.*
*   **Transmutation:** Altering physical properties. *Polymorph, Haste, Feather Fall.*

### Spell Attributes & Geometry
*   **Components:**
    *   **V (Verbal):** Chanting. Silence status prevents this.
    *   **S (Somatic):** Gestures. Requires one free hand.
    *   **M (Material):** Physical object. If no cost listed, Component Pouch suffies. If Cost listed (e.g. 50gp diamond), item is required.
    *   **Consumption:** If spell says "consume", item is destroyed.
*   **Area of Effect (AoE) on Grid:**
    *   **Cone:** Origin corner to width = length. (Standard 5-3-5 templates).
    *   **Cube:** Origin on face or corner. Full coverage.
    *   **Line:** Straight line from origin. Width usually 5ft.
    *   **Sphere/Radius:** Origin intersection. Distance calculated Chebyshev (max(dx, dy)) or Euclidean.
