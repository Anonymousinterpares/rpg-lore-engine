# Content: Feats

## 1. Alert
*   +5 to Initiative.
*   Can't be surprised while conscious.
*   Other creatures don't gain Advantage on attack rolls against you as a result of being unseen by you.

## 2. Athlete
*   Increase STR or DEX by 1 (max 20).
*   Standing up from prone uses only 5 ft of movement.
*   Climbing doesn't cost extra movement.
*   Running jump requires only 5 ft preparation.

## 3. Actor
*   Increase CHA by 1 (max 20).
*   Advantage on Deception/Performance when trying to pass as another person.
*   Can mimic speech/sounds (Insight vs Deception to detect).

## 4. Great Weapon Master
*   On Crit or Kill, take a melee weapon attack as Bonus Action.
*   Before melee attack with Heavy weapon, you can choose to take -5 penalty to Attack Roll for +10 Damage.

## 5. Lucky
*   You have 3 Luck Points.
*   Whenever you make an Attack, Check, or Save, you can spend 1 point to roll an additional d20 and choose which to use.
*   Regain consumed points on Long Rest.

## 6. Mobile
*   Speed increases by 10 ft.
*   Dash on difficult terrain doesn't cost extra movement.
*   When you make a melee attack (hit or miss), you don't provoke Opportunity Attacks from that creature for the rest of the turn.

## 7. Polearm Master
*   When taking Attack action with Glaive/Halberd/Staff, can use Bonus Action to attack with butt end (1d4 Bludgeoning).
*   Provoke Opportunity Attack when entering reach.

## 8. Resilient
*   Increase one Ability Score by 1.
*   Gain Proficiency in Saving Throws using that ability.

## 9. Sentinel
*   Hit on Opportunity Attack reduces creature speed to 0.
*   Creatures provoke Opportunity Attacks even on Disengage.
*   Reaction to attack creature within 5 ft attacking target other than you.

## 10. Sharpshooter
*   Attacking at long range doesn't impose Disadvantage.
*   Weapons ignore 1/2 and 3/4 cover.
*   Before attack with ranged weapon, can choose -5 penalty to Attack for +10 Damage.

## 11. War Caster
*   Advantage on CON saves to maintain concentration.
*   Can perform somatic components with weapons/shield in hand.
*   Cast spell (1 Action, single target) as Reaction opportunity attack.

## 12. Ability Score Improvement
*   Increase one Ability Score by 2, or two Scores by 1. (Max 20).
