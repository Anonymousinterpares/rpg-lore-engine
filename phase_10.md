Plan: Phase 10 — Skill Improvement & Progression System

Status: PLACEHOLDER — To be brainstormed and developed when Phase 10 begins.

---

Overview

Skills (Arcana, Investigation, Survival, etc.) are currently binary — a character either has proficiency or doesn't.
Phase 10 introduces a skill point investment system where players can improve individual skills over time,
unlocking stronger effects, more daily uses, and new capabilities.

---

Core Concepts (to be expanded)

Skill Ranks
- Skills gain ranks through invested skill points (e.g., Rank 1-5)
- Higher ranks unlock more powerful or frequent uses of skill-gated actions
- Example: Arcana Rank 1 = 1 identification attempt per 24h; Rank 3 = 3 attempts per 24h

Scaling Effects per Rank
- More daily actions (identification attempts, tracking uses, etc.)
- Improved DCs / success chances (bonus to skill checks)
- Longer duration or stronger effects for skill-based abilities
- New sub-abilities unlocked at milestone ranks

Skill Points
- Source: level-up, quest rewards, training, or downtime activities
- Allocation: player chooses which skills to invest in
- Cost scaling: higher ranks cost more points

---

Affected Systems (preliminary)

- Item Identification (Arcana/Investigation): daily attempt count scales with rank
- Survival: foraging, tracking, camp quality
- Perception: trap/secret detection range/reliability
- Persuasion/Intimidation/Deception: social encounter modifiers
- Medicine: healing during rest, poison/disease treatment
